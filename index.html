About MiiTravels

MiiTravels allows you to remember all the trips you have been on with utmost detail. You can note down your trips and their associated locations. In addition, you can view your trip data in a structured manner through the dashboard, as well as through a map where all trips are represented by trip pins. You can also share your trips with your friends if you wish. With MiiTravels, you will never forget the details with regards to your trips.

Privacy Policy

MiiTravels in no way collects your data, nor shares it with third party applications and services. All your data remains private to you and is stored in your iCloud account.
